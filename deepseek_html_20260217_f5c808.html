PROJECT: ECHOES OF ATLAS

CLIENT: ASTRA GAMES | CHALLENGE: Build a mythos that feels ancient and new.

SEED

The initial brief: "Create a world where mythology meets quantum physics. The gods are not beings, but algorithms that shaped reality."

"What if consciousness is just a particularly elegant code running on the hardware of the universe?" - Client brief excerpt

CONVERGENCE

Mentor session with KAI ZHANG: "The mythos needs rules. If gods are algorithms, what are their inputs? What are their outputs?"

"We developed the 'Recursive Pantheon' concept - each deity is a function that calls other deities, creating an infinite hierarchy of divine code." - Session notes

ITERATION

Three visual concepts presented: "Circuitry Glyphs", "Dataflow Mandalas", and "Quantum Tapestry". The client was drawn to the tension between organic and digital in "Quantum Tapestry".

REFINEMENT

The worldbuilding bible expands to 200 pages. Characters are defined not by lineage but by their "access privileges" to reality's source code.

REALIZATION

The final game trailer launches. Viewer retention is 40% above industry average. The mythos becomes the foundation for a transmedia franchise.
